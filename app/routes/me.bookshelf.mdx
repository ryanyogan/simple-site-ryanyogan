
# My Bookshelf

These are the books, videos, and courses that I find to be my favorite and highly relevant to date.




## Books

- **[Peopleware: Productive Projects & Teams](https://www.amazon.com/Peopleware-Productive-Projects-Tom-DeMarco/dp/0932633439)**
  - Author: Tom DeMarco and Timothy Lister
  - Genre: Software Development Management
  - My Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
  - Brief thoughts: This book offers invaluable insights into the human aspects of software development. It's a must-read for anyone in tech leadership, providing practical advice on creating productive and happy teams.

- **[Engineering Management for the Rest of Us](https://www.engmanagement.dev/)**
  - Author: Sarah Drasner
  - Genre: Engineering Management
  - My Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
  - Brief thoughts: Sarah's approach is refreshingly practical and accessible. This book is perfect for those of us who weren't "born leaders" but are committed to becoming effective engineering managers.

## Courses
